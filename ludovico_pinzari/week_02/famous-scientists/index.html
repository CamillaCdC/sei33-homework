Welcome to my Page

I'm an American computer scientist and Turing award winner (1974). I'm well known for my major contributions to the analysis of algorithms and the design of programming languages, and in particular for my contributions to the "Art of computer programming" book series. The collections of technique, algorithms, and relevant theory in these books have served as a focal point for developing curricula and as an organizing influence on computer science.

I saved the life of many scientists and Ph.D students by creating TEX. A free typsetting program for the communication and publication of scientific documents.
